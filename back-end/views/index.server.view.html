ABOUT US

Let's plan your dream day!

LOVELAND is able to accommodate a wide range of event planning needs by offering different levels of service from coast to coast. Each service is centered around making the planning process an enjoyable journey with guidance from a seasoned professional from start to finish. Regardless of the service you select, you can rest assured that our refined process will save you time, energy, and most importantly allow you the luxury of enjoying this joyful time with the one you love.

Our tried-and-true approach has proven successful with over 100 events to date. As our relationship as your trusted planning partner develops, we get to know you on a more personal level. We learn what you value most and where you place your priorities. Whether you desire an inviting and warm environment for a 250-person black tie affair or a more intimate event at one of the top wedding destinations in the world, our goal is to provide a seamless and blissful experience for our clients, their families, and guests.
